What Are They

Peonies are vibrant beauties that are generally seen for a gloriously short amount of time in spring.

Varieties

There are hundreds of different types of peonies and each has unique and breathtaking flowers. There are Tree peonies, Herbaceous peonies, Itoh peonies, Woodland peonies, and peonies from different regions have different styled leaves and flowers as well.

Seasons & Range

Most peonies bloom in springtime, but some do bloom in summer as well. They are like daffodils quick to bloom, but vibrant to behold. Surprisingly peonies do well in cold climates and bloom better and longer when cooler. There zones fall around 2-9.

Care

Peonies are not tricky to care for. They often need to have their flowers propped because they are often too large to stand on their own. They are susceptible to fungi and other pests so care must be taken to dispose of old leaves every fall (not tree peonies) to keep peonies healthy. They also may need parasols to protect them from the sun, since they like a little shade. Ants love peonies, but don't worry sugar ants are not a pest and should be left alone.
